Re: Request for Permission to Conduct MuMent - Evening Club

Dear Sir,

I hope this letter finds you in good health and high spirits. I am writing to seek your permission to establish an evening club named MuMent under the auspices of MuLearn STI at our esteemed institution, St. Thomas Institute of Science and Technology.

Overview

MuMent is an initiative where students can come together to form small learning circles, consisting of a maximum of five members each. These circles will be based on shared interests, enabling participants to upskill in their chosen domains through a self-learning approach. The primary platform for this initiative will be the MuLearn STI Discord channel, which will facilitate communication and coordination among members.

Learning and Assessment

Participants will be required to show proof of their work and progress through the Discord channel. Each task submitted will be verified by a designated mentor, and upon approval, participants will earn Karma points. These points will serve as a motivational tool and a measure of each student's engagement and progress.

Request for Approval

In light of the above, I kindly request your permission to conduct MuMent at our institute. I firmly believe that this initiative will foster a collaborative learning environment, enhance skill development, and ultimately contribute to the holistic growth of our students.

I am happy to provide any further information you might require and discuss this proposal in more detail at your convenience.

Thank you for considering this request.
